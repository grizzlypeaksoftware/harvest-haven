Harvest Haven

How to Play

  • Click on the tool buttons at the top of the game to select your tool.
  • Use the shovel tool to prepare the soil for planting.
  • Plant seeds in the prepared soil using the seed tool.
  • Water your crops regularly using the watering can tool.
  • Harvest your crops when they're fully grown using the scythe tool.
  • Sell your harvested crops using the sell buttons in the inventory.
  • Buy more seeds as you earn money.
  • Watch out for the changing seasons and plan your crops accordingly!
